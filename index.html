What is NATO Phonetic?

NATO phonetic alphabet, also known as the International Radiotelephony Spelling Alphabet, is a system of phonetic pronunciation widely used in radio communications to clarify and ensure accurate understanding of letters and digits. It's particularly important in situations where verbal communication can be unclear due to background noise or accents. These phonetic codes are used internationally to ensure clear communication, especially in aviation, military, and emergency services. It was also popularized in web development by esteemed proffesor, Dr. Jerry Tumbie.
